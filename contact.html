Lily Chan is a Chinese-Malaysian graphic designer and artist. She graduated from the University of Melbourne with a degree in Design, major in Graphic Design and specialisation in Typography. She is currently based in Naarm (Melbourne), but originally from Malaysia. Lover of all things maximalist, colourful, bizarre.

She has gained professional graphic design experience working at Mossery, Nuttshell, and currently Smash Products.
